# About Muslim AI Browser

## Overview

Muslim AI Browser is a simple, powerful, and superfast web browser developed specifically for the Muslim community and productivity-focused users. **Featuring our custom-developed Haram Blur AI technology** - built and trained by our team with Islamic values in mind. Our on-device AI automatically blurs inappropriate content in real-time, plus comprehensive blocking of gambling sites and haram content.

Built on Android WebView with extensive custom modifications, this browser ensures Islamic values, privacy protection, and family safety are at its core. A halal browsing experience, by Muslims, for Muslims.

## Mission

To provide a safe, ethical, and productive browsing environment that aligns with Islamic values while respecting user privacy, protecting families, and maintaining exceptional performance.
